Introduction

The field of computer vision has emerged as a transformative force, empowering machines to comprehend and interpret visual data from images and videos. This project specifically centers on applying bird detection in images utilizing the YOLOv3 model. Birds play a crucial role in ecosystems, and understanding their populations and behaviors is important for conservation efforts and ecological studies. The implementation of bird detection, facilitated by the YOLOv3 model, will aid conservationists in identifying birds amidst diverse environmental conditions. The dataset used for the project contains diverse images with various backgrounds, lighting conditions, and bird species. Apart from showcasing the technical capabilities of computer vision algorithms, this project emphasizes their practical significance in advancing wildlife monitoring and ornithology research.

Method

    Our goal is to detect the number of birds in the given picture. We chose YoloV3 for this task.

  1. Step 1 Image Collection: We began by gathering a set of pictures featuring diverse bird scenarios. These images encompassed different lighting conditions, backgrounds, and scenes.
  2. Step 2 Preprocessing: After collecting images, we applied preprocessing techniques to improve dataset quality. This involved tasks like resizing and normalization to optimize images for model input.
  3. Step 3 Model Selection: We chose the YOLOv3 model, pre-trained on a general object detection dataset. YOLOv3's capability to detect multiple objects in real-time made it suitable for our bird counting task.
  4. Step 4 Processing: Deploying YOLOv3 in the inference phase, the model analyzed bird dataset images, generating bounding boxes around detected birds. This process provided bird counts and spatial information.
  5. Step 5 Postprocessing: To refine results, we implemented a postprocessing algorithm. This step addressed challenges like overlapping bounding boxes and varied bird sizes, aiming for an accurate bird count considering detection complexities
